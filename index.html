Frequently Asked Questions

What is the time commitment?

The program runs for 6 weeks, with sessions held every Saturday for 2.5 to 3 hours. We also recommend setting aside 2-3 hours per week for homework and content creation.

What if I miss a live session?

No problem. We offer a hybrid model where all live in-person sessions are recorded. You will have lifetime access to the recording portal to catch up at your own pace.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes. We want you to be confident in your decision. Refunds are available up until the start of Session 2. No refunds are issued after the first week to ensure commitment to the cohort.

Absolutely. Upon successful completion of the 6 weeks and your final capstone project, you will receive an official "Content Creator Masterclass" certificate from The Mayor Agency.
